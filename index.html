This coffeeshop is located in the hollowed out husk of a mortal cathedral, copied by Mab's miraculous reality-scribes before the humans let it get damaged by one of their many historical events. We have a fiduciary relationship with the Gallery of Colours - provide us with your ticket to any of their recent events and you'll get a discount! And the advantage of taking part in the mortal ritual of morality known as 'recycling'. All our recipes come from the widest variety of human establishments we could access, to ensure you get the most vivid experiences from beyond our dimension.

We can be found only by imbibing the squid-schema and staring into a mirror while chanting the requisite block-C0DAs.

We lost several of our staff to hunting-nobles atop their stellar-doe steeds. If you wish to apply, twist three times and screech into the void.

Karaoke Night

Source

This event is a regular mortal tradition, all about making noise to a preset 'track' of noise while words go by on a screen. Their demon-of-many-voices defines is thusly: Karaoke (n.) is a socially sanctioned ritual wherein otherwise rational humans voluntarily simulate vocal cord gymnastics over instrumental tracks, often in dimly lit venues, while wielding a microphone like a wizard’s wand of delusion. A performance art form powered by liquid courage and the unwavering belief that the world needs to hear your rendition of Bohemian Rhapsody. We aren't sure what this means, but it fits that horrifying mortal whimsy and promises an experience unlike any you'll forget.

The event will happen at twelve twists of the Heliocentric, to give us time to set up the obligatory stage, hovering fire-script, and fairy lights. The Historians tell us those are a cultural requirement of this ritual. If you wish to participate, please click the button below to express interest and leave your name and email for updates.
