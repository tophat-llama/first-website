This coffeeshop is located in the hollowed out husk of Notre Dame Cathedral, copied by Mab's miraculous reality-scribes before the humans let it get damaged by fire. We have a fiduciary relationship with the Gallery of Colours - provide us with your ticket to any of their recent events and you'll get a discount! And the advantage of taking part in the mortal ritual of morality known as 'recycling'. All our recipes come from the widest variety of human establishments we could access, to ensure you get the most vivid experiences from beyond our dimension.

We can be found only by imbibing the squid-schema and staring into a mirror while chanting the requisite block-C0DAs.

We lost several of our staff to hunting-nobles atop their stellar-doe steeds. If you wish to apply, twist three times and screech into the void.
